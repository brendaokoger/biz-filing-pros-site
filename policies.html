03 — Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our refund and cancellation policies vary depending on the type of service purchased. Please review the full policy for important details.

Refund and cancellation terms vary by service type. Please review the applicable policy below before submitting an order or payment. Government filing fees, third-party vendor costs, and agency fees are generally non-refundable once submitted or incurred.

Business Formation Cancellation Policy

Your order is refundable until payment is forwarded to the state, typically within twenty-four hours after your order is placed, less a $30.00 cancellation fee and less any expenses paid or incurred in furtherance of your order with any entities, including state agencies or third-party vendors.

Once payment has been forwarded to the state, Biz Filing Pros LLC cannot accept cancellations or changes to the state filing. We typically forward your filing and payment to the state within twenty-four hours after your order is placed.

If you have placed an order and would like to cancel it, you must send an email to support@bizfilingpros.com. Cancellation requests or changes to an order cannot be accepted by telephone.

Biz Filing Pros LLC operates exclusively at the client's direction and does not offer legal, tax, or accounting advice or services. No information provided by Biz Filing Pros LLC constitutes legal, tax, or accounting advice.

Nonprofit Formation Cancellation Policy & IRS Review Notice

Biz Filing Pros LLC provides nonprofit formation and federal tax-exempt application preparation services based on the information provided by the client. Due to the nature of state and federal filings, the following cancellation and review terms apply.

Orders may be canceled prior to submission of any documents or payments to state or federal agencies. If a cancellation request is received before submission, a refund of Biz Filing Pros' professional fees will be issued, less a $30 processing fee and less any third-party costs already incurred.

Once any documents or payments have been submitted to a state agency, the Internal Revenue Service, or any other government authority, all sales are final and no refunds will be issued.

Government fees, including but not limited to the IRS Form 1023 user fee, state nonprofit incorporation fees, and any charitable registration fees, are paid directly to the appropriate agencies and are non-refundable once submitted, regardless of application outcome.

IRS Review, Denials & Additional Information Requests

Approval of nonprofit incorporation and federal tax-exempt status is determined solely by the Internal Revenue Service and applicable state agencies. Biz Filing Pros LLC does not guarantee approval of any nonprofit or tax-exempt application.

If the IRS issues a request for additional information, clarification, or documentation related to the submitted application, the client agrees to cooperate and provide the requested information in a timely manner. Reasonable assistance in responding to standard IRS inquiries related to the original filing is included.

If an application is denied, delayed, or requires substantial revision due to information provided by the client, changes in organizational activities, or failure to meet IRS eligibility requirements, no refunds will be issued.

Additional services beyond the scope of the original filing, including but not limited to application amendments, re-filings, appeals, or restructuring, are not included and may require additional fees.

Cancellation Requests

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to support@bizfilingpros.com. Cancellation requests cannot be accepted by phone.

By submitting an application and proceeding with services, the client acknowledges and agrees to this Cancellation Policy and IRS Review Notice.
